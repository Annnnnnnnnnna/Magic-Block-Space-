Magic Block Space

How to Play

  • You control a wizard with your mouse cursor.
  • You can also move with W A S D or the Arrow Keys.
  • Avoid the falling meteorites at all costs.
  • Catch the Magic bonus to gain score.
  • Whenever you collect a Magic bonus, all meteorites currently visible on the screen disappear in a big explosion.
  • The game is endless, so try to survive as long as possible and collect as many Magic bonuses as you can.
  • The longer you survive, the faster and more dangerous the meteorites become.
  • If a meteorite hits the wizard, the game ends immediately.
  • After the game ends, your final result is shown and your best score is saved automatically.
